Design Decision Summary
What this UI Kit gives us vs. what the current design has.
Aspect Current Design (Glassy Dark) Appetit Design (Warm Brown)
Vibe Tech/futuristic, glass morphism Warm, cookbook, artisanal
Background #0A0A0F (cold black) #1A1208 (warm brown-black)
Accent #FF6B35 (coral) #E8832A (amber/orange)
Cards Glass blur + opacity layers Full-bleed photo with gradient overlay
Food Focus Medium — UI-forward Maximum — food photography is the hero
Light Mode Not built yet Full dual-mode (cream/warm white)
"Cookbook" Feel Not really Yes — exactly what Nicolas asked for (#17)
Screens Ready ~8 screens built 63 screens x 2 modes = 126 variants
